2. Search laundromats

1) You can search for laundromats by entering a few words into the search box, and we'll show you all the matching results.

3. Create an order

1) Select Laundromat after you've reviewed all laundromats in the list.
2) Enter an address, choose visiting time, and enter your payment information.
3) Review your order details.
4) Click Place your order

4. Write a review

1) Go to the orders detail page for the review on bubl.io.
2) Click Write a customer review in the Customer Reviews section.
3) Wright your review and click Save.
